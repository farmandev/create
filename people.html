Our People

Deepti Sawhney

CEO & Founder

Deepti has been an educator, having worked in schools in India and the UK. Winner of the Microsoft Innovative Teachers’ Leadership Award, she represented India at Seoul in 2005. She completed her Certificate of Educational Studies from The College of Teachers, Institute of Education, London, UK and has trained in Teaching Languages through Technology from Trinity College, UK with a Distinction Grade in her ICT project from Consultant-E, Spain. She was the Leader for ICT at the British School for six years and has been a teacher educator for Technology Integration for the last 15 years now.

Before setting up Mahattattva, she worked as Head, ICT Initiatives, Learn Today, Learning Division of the India Today Group where she was responsible for providing consultations to schools on technology integration, she spearheaded technology initiatives in all the projects that Learn Today undertook and also conducted teacher development programmes in technology integration (India and Bhutan). She also wrote the technology curriculum for the teacher resource book for The Royal Academy, Bhutan.

She is currently pursuing her PhD in Educational Technology and working with Google India on NCERT projects for teacher training.

In March 2019, she was awarded the BETT Asia Leadership Award at Kuala Lumpur for her work on ‘Design Thinking for Social Change’ project. This project is globally recognised and has been made into a case-study by UPenn, US.

Vidur Sawhney

M.D & Co-Founder

Vidur is a commerce graduate from the University of Delhi. He leads a team of educators and academics at Mahattattva as the Managing Director. Before taking up this role, he has been associated with some of the largest professional consultancies in the world, such as Deloitte and KPMG.

He has worked with several Fortune 500 companies during his stint in the corporate world. Vidur has also spearheaded multi-crore pursuits for his employers, helping expand their footprint in India.

His core expertise is in research, analytics, client acquisition, client relationship management and marketing.

He strives to incorporate global best practices in consulting at Mahattattva.

Usha Pathania

Director — Academics

Usha has been passionately involved in various aspects of education for over 30 years. She is committed to professional ethics, maintaining exacting standards of educactional practices and the care and education of young children As a teacher she was associated with Presentation Convent, Army Schools, and Cambridge Foundation School, Delhi where she constantly strived to have an engaged classroom.

Her experience in theatre and as a debater, her love for music and her vivacious personality, combined with her domain expertise, revolutionised her classrooms.

This was recognised when she received the ‘Excellence in Teaching’ Award by Career Launcher. Her exposure to innovations in the field grew as a result of several school exchange programmes initiated by the British Council.

Presently, she is the Director -Academics at Mahattattva. Her diverse experience and strong track record in fostering child-centered curriculum and student creativity adds lustre to her luminous career. She also looks into the academic audits of schools and sets up new schools.

Mitsu has been in the teaching profession for over twenty five years. She graduated with an honours degree from Miranda House College in 1982 and completed her teacher’s training from Department of Education, Delhi University the following year. She gained her Master’s degree from Agra University in 1998. She began her teaching career at Laxman Public School, Delhi when it was still a budding institution. A transfer to Jodhpur led her to St Anne’s School where the pedagogy was classical but was juxtaposed with the freedom to experiment. Army School Agra was the next challenge as the learners were first generation, straight out of their villages. Next came Springdales School, Dhaula Kuan, which follows a child centric pedagogy with ABL and PBL. Her experience with ICSC came at Greenlawns School, Mumbai. The move back to Delhi in 2015 led to a shift in her career from school teaching to teacher support. With Learning Links Foundation she designed curriculum and content to support their efforts with government schools across the country. For a short period, she headed their intervention with a MCD school at Delhi. She then joined Universal Learn Today where she supported schools through direct intervention as well as teacher training workshops. Mitsu has always strived to challenge herself professionally as well as personally. She did a course with INTEL when computers were emerging as tools in the education space. She did a beginner’s course in French to experience functional learning of a foreign language. She applied this learning to her classrooms with excellent results.

Sonali Bhatt,

Academic Coordinator, Early Years Specialist

Sonali has over 18 years of varied experience in the field of education. A post graduate from Maharaja Sayajirao University, Baroda, she completed her teacher’s training from Benaras Hindu University, Varanasi. Being an Army officer’s wife, she has widely traversed the length and breadth of the country taking up teaching assignments. She has experienced working in a multitude of schools ranging from Army, Navy, Convents and Sadhbhawana Schools. At the Sadhbhawana School, Leh, she introduced the concept of Parent’s Orientation at the beginning of each academic session. She was the Principal of the Army Preschool, Ahmedabad which challenged her to manage both academics as well as administration. Her last teaching assignment was with Vasant Valley School, New Delhi where she implemented strategies to accommodate differentiated learning thereby enabling self learning in children while inculcating strong reading habits. At Mahattattva, Sonali will be assisting and providing guidance in setting up new schools for early years including mentoring teachers and head(s) on content, curriculum, modules, usage and applicability. She will also monitor implementation of developed content and techniques.

Ashima Jairath

Academic Coordinator

An award-winning Principal, Ashima Jairath is a B.Sc. (Hons) graduate from Delhi University specialising in Child Development and Psychology. She is an experienced educational facilitator, curriculum developer, content creator and teacher trainer, with over twenty years of experience in prestigious academic institutions of the country, Vasant Valley; DPS Jalandhar; GD Goenka Vasant Kunj to name a few and brings to the table the best practices she has garnered so far. A dynamic leader, she is proactive, innovative and passionate about education and believes that empowering teachers is the best way to move the dial on student improvement. Armed with great interpersonal skills, she is an effective communicator and presenter. Her forte lies in providing guidance and leadership to staff through grooming, training and mentoring to building powerful teams. She works towards enhancing the teaching-learning process to go beyond textbooks, to make students and teachers “future-ready” to face challenges that the real-world poses through student-centred learning and individualized instruction, experiential learning interventions and a multidisciplinary, hands-on activity-based approach involving multiple intelligences, using encouragement and positive reinforcement as tools to aid learning. She has designed and implemented innovative customised age-appropriate educational content and Report Cards; developed curriculum & content for a Nursery Teachers Training Course for Nalanda Learning Systems Pvt Ltd, Kolkata; provided audio voiceover support for Math tutorials (Classes IV to VII) for Second School Learning Pvt. Ltd. and authored Math Workbooks- Grade 1 to 5 for Arya Book Depot. An avid animal lover and travel buff, she enjoys reading, painting and solving word and number puzzles.

Kajal Kapur

Executive Assistant to the MD

She lives with her husband and 13-year-old daughter in Gurgaon, India. She is a writer, a writing coach, an avid reader, a movie buff and a social media enthusiast.

RAJESH SOOD

Chairman of The Advisory Board

An IIM (L) and SRCC alumnus, Mr Sood has a stellar career of over 26 years in the Banking, financial services and insurance (BFSI) space. He has worked extensively on Pan India portfolios across Banking, Consumer Finance and Insurance. He has also been involved with setting up and running operations in Asia Pacific.

He has been associated with international giants such as ANZ Grindlays Bank, GE Capital, Bank of America and DHFL Pramerica. He presently serves as EVP and Chief Distribution Officer at DHFL Pramerica, helping establish it's presence amongst the Top 10 Life Insurers of India.

Mr Sood is a key investor at Mahattattva, and as the Chairman of the Advisory Board, he empowers the company with a wealth of experience that he has gained in his many years in the corporate sector.

Mike Knowles FRSA

Principal Advisor

Professor Knowles started his professional career training to teach 3D design at Southfields College, London. After entering into industry, he worked with many leading London designers before setting up his first studio, Mike Knowles and Associates in London in the mid eighties. A ‘ Ruskinite ‘ he is a passionate believer in the societal value of design and craft skills. Professor Knowles moved to India to develop job creation and export projects and helped establish the In Group. In 2003, he was elected a Fellow of the Royal Society of Arts and up until recently headed the India Fellowship. In 2008, he established a Skills Academy for the University of the Arts, London in New Delhi and was conferred a Visiting Professor UAL in 2009. In 2010, he was given the responsibility of developing the Sushant School of Design where he was Dean for 5 years, growing the school from a handful of students to one of India’s leading Design Schools. He now heads the Creative Hub, SSD at Ansal University.

Yvonne Harrison

Technology Advisor

Yvonne Harrison is qualified as a Primary Teacher with a Masters Degree in Science Education (ICT applied in the classroom) and a Graduate Certificate in Gifted Education. Before becoming an administrator, Yvonne was a Level 3 Classroom Teacher (2001), Microsoft Innovative Educator (2005), and Getting It Right Literacy Specialist teacher (2005). Her current role has a specific focus on Digital Technologies, Gifted and Talented Students, and Early Childhood Development. She has been teaching for well over 30 years and often uses ICT as a vehicle for student learning projects across the curriculum. Yvonne is currently working alongside staff members at her school, and in conjunction with staff from neighbouring schools in the catchment cluster, to assist in the implementation of robotics and coding lessons to aid in the 2018 moderation and reporting of Digital Technologies in the primary school. She has presented at ECAWA State Conferences from 2001, Department of Education Innovation and ICT Seminars, Microsoft Roadshow, TeachMeets and now a STEM Conference incorporating the professional STEM associations of Western Australia. Her volunteer work includes running an after school Computer Club using a multiplayer Minecraft Server and an after school CoderDojo wherein students are able to follow their own passions in game making, animation creation, coding and simple VR development.

Sangeeta Gulati
